Pearl

Ruby

Everything with Pearl plus:

  • Obtain a three hour consultation
  • Up to ten hours of orchestration at the wedding and reception

  • We will track the fitting and payments of your wedding party
  • Provide refreshments and toiletries to wedding party

Diamond

Everything with Gold plus:

  • Communicate with and schedule the wedding party
  • Instruct wedding party throughout the planning
